### ✨ Upload a selfie. Know your face with AI. ✨

</div>

a free AI analysis of their face, and can unlock a complete **Clinical Luxury
profile** — a precise, personalized report on beauty, facial harmony, and a
recommended care journey.

> A selfie goes in. A clinical-grade aesthetic profile comes out — beautifully,

## 🔒 Privacy by Design

Privacy is the core of the product. Images are kept in private storage, access
happens only through secure short-lived links, and sign-in is protected by
mobile one-time codes.

> Your face never becomes public. Storage stays private, access is short-lived,
> and authentication is OTP-based.
